school achievements

Top CBSE School - for "Holistic Education"

Delhi Public School Electronic City is Ranked No.1 in India under the Top CBSE School - for "Holistic Education" in a survey conducted by EducationToday.

International School Award

Delhi Public School, Electronic City is proud to receive acknowledgement in the form of the coveted International School Award 2017-2020 from the British Council , for its international dimension in the curriculum.

Global Innovative Schools Awards - GISA 2018

DPS ELECTRONIC CITY has been conferred with the GLOBAL INNOVATIVE SCHOOLS AWARD - GISA 2018 for the exemplary performance in academics, technology integrated learning, cultural inclusion and creating social awareness

Behtar India Award

DPS Electronic City is committed to Behtar India Campaign that. The campaign aims at providing possible solutions for a Behtar vatavaran(Environment), Behtar Swachhata(Hygiene) and Behtar Swasthya(Health). The school won the ‘Behtar India Award’ for DPS Electronic City for successful completion of last year’s campaign.
